The Story

Hi, I'm Alex.
I obsess over details.

I'm a product designer and frontend developer based in San Francisco. I've spent the last 8 years at the intersection of design and engineering — building products that millions of people use every day.

My approach is simple: start with people, end with pixels. I believe great design is invisible — it just works, and users never have to think about it.

When I'm not pushing pixels, I'm rock climbing, tinkering with side projects, or hunting for the world's best espresso.

What I Believe

Design Principles

01

Clarity over cleverness

The smartest solution is the one anyone can understand. I strip away complexity until only the essential remains.

02

Systems thinking

Good design scales. I build reusable systems and components that make teams faster and products more consistent.

03

Empathy first

Every interaction is a conversation with a real person. I design for the stressed, the tired, and the first-time user.

04

Craft matters

The difference between good and great is in the details nobody notices consciously but everyone feels.
